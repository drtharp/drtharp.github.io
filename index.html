Cut Carbon. Pay People.

A simple policy: make pollution cost something—and return the money to you.

How it works

Fossil fuel companies pay a fee based on the carbon dioxide their fuels will emit. The money is returned equally to Americans as a monthly dividend.

  • Pollution is no longer free
  • Clean energy becomes more competitive
  • You receive a direct payment

What it means for you

Most households come out ahead.

  • Lower-emitting households receive more than they pay
  • Higher emitters pay more
  • Lower-income households benefit most

Why now

Climate-related costs are rising, and businesses need predictable signals. A carbon fee provides a clear, consistent incentive to reduce emissions.
